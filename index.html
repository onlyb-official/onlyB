WHAT IS ONLY B?

ONLY B is a custom art brand created by university designers. Instead of mass production, we transform ordinary objects into unique artworks. Sneakers. Headphones. Bags. Clothing. Every line is drawn by hand. No copies. No printing. Only one. Only B.

THE IDEA

The world is constantly changing. Our designs represent chain reactions, movement, and the power to create change with your own hands. Black and white. Simple at first glance. But every person discovers something different.
